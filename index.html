About Me

I have great interest in coding, I would say my stronges attribute is web development. I have intermediate proficiency in C, C++, Java, I am really good in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, Express and MongoDB, other than that I am also handy with some of the frameworks and libraries like Bootstrap, Wordpress, jQuery, React. I am also familiar with Git, GitHub and PUG.

I have great interest in football, volleyball and badminton, I have also represented my school at CBSE zonal level football competition. I also exel in singing and art and craft.
